Very Best Friend?

The right hand rule is your Very Best Friend. (q)v x B = F.
V, B, F. Thumb, index, middle. Very Best Friend.
Thumb out in the plane of your palm, pointer up also in the plane of your palm so it is 90 degrees from your thumb, and middle perpendicular to the plane of your palm.
This is a great way to remember what's going on in magnetism classes (like AP PhysC E&M) where you've got to remember the best gang sign of them all:
RHR, your Very Best Friend. (This was invented by me!)


Don't Procrastinate!

Well? Are you procrastinating? Don't!
Are you doing things right on time? Don't!
Are you finishing things wayyyyy before they are due to allow a little buffer time period in case you get sick or the randomest thing happens?
Good. That's a work ethic that you will be recognized for and rewarded for.
